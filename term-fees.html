Quick Help

Term Fees

Every new term you will need to recreate the term fees for that term.
 
There are five different price options : 
  • Fixed Price 
    Fixed term price for every member of the class it is applied to.
  • Pro Rata Monthly or Weekly 
    A relative price applied to members joining part way through the term. Each week/month elapsed since the start of the term, the amount will reduce by a proportion according to the total number of weeks in the term (rounded up). Note - you must select 'By Week' or 'By Month' depending on your preferences.
  • Per Session 
    A fee automatically calculated based on the remaining attendance sessions in that term. E.g: if the fee is $10 per session and there are 10 attendances remaining the customer will be charged $100.
  • Monthly 
    A fee applied monthly. Members will be charged monthly the amount entered into the ‘Amount’ field. 
  • Nil Fee
    No fee.

Add a Term Fee

  1. Hover on 'Fees'.
  2. Click 'Term Fees'.
  3. The top panel is where you create new term fees.

  4. Select the term you want to apply the fee for.
  5. Select the classes you are applying the fee to. If the same fee applies to multiple classes you can select as many as you like.   
    Note - you may not be able to select some classes as you already have a term fee applied to them - you can only have one term fee per term per class.
  6. If you have Xero enabled you may be asked to choose your fee category. 
  7. Choose a fee price option - Refer above for more.
  8. Set the due date or days.
  9. Enter the amount. 
  10. Click 'Add'.
    .
    If the the button is not clickable this means the data has not been filled in correctly.

  11. As you create term fees they will then appear in the table below.
Note - There may be different options based on the fee option you have selected.  

Apply a Term Fee

Term fees can be applied in multiple ways. 

Player Registering
The first way and the most common way a fee will be applied iswhen someone is registering for the term. As they go through the process and choose a class, the fee will be applied automatically. 

Term fees page
The second way from the 'Term Fees' page.

  1. Hover on 'Fees'.
  2. Click 'Term Fees'.
  3. Find the fee you want to apply. 
  4. Click 'Apply'.
  5. You will be presented with a pop up which will list all people part of the class that have not received a fee yet.
  6. Simply go through the list and click the check boxes for the people that you want to apply the term fee to.
  7. Click 'Apply' - The fee has now been applied.
Adding someone to a class
The third way that a term fee can be applied is when you're adding someone to a class that has a term fee applied to it. - Click here for more details. 

Apply a Monthly Term Fee

  1. Hover on 'Fees'. 
  2. Click 'Term Fees'. 
  3. Find the fee you want to apply. 
  4. Click 'Apply'. 
  5. You will be presented with a pop up which will list all people part of the class that have not received a fee yet.
  6. Simply go through the list and click the check boxes for the people that you want to apply the term fee to.
  7. Click 'Apply' - The fee has now been applied. 
Note - The monthly fee will be applied to the next recurring date set for that specific term fee. You cannot backdate when applying a monthly fee. 

Edit a Term Fee

  1. Hover on 'Fees'. 
  2. Click 'Term Fees'. 
  3. Locate the term fee in the 'Term Fees' table that you would like to edit.
  4. Click the blue edit icon at the end of the row.
  5. Edit the details.
  6. Click the green save icon.

Edit a Monthly Term Fee for a Specific Member

This editing process can be used for specific members and is helpful if you want to put a monthly fee on hold.


  1. Hover on 'Fees'. 
  2. Click 'Term Fees'. 
  3. Locate the fee in the 'Term Fees' table that you would like to edit.
  4. In the 'Applied To' column next to the 'Apply' button click 'people'. 
    Note - If the fee hasn't been applied to any members you will need to apply it first.
  5. Find the specific member and in the 'Date' column click the blue recurring symbol.
  6. You can choose to change the 'Amount', 'Next Date' and 'End Date' or click 'Stop Recur' to stop the monthly fee for that individual.
  7. Click 'Save'.

Delete a Term Fee

  1. Hover on 'Fees'.
  2. Click 'Term Fees'.
  3. Locate the term fee in the 'Term Fees' table that you would like to delete.
  4. Click the delete icon at the end of the row.   
  5. A popup will appear. 
  6. Click 'Delete'. 
Note - The fee will not be deleted off the account of members that have already had the fee applied to them.

Term Fee Discount

You have the option to create 'Registration Discounts'. These discounts will be applied when members register depending on the discount requirements you set. 

Registration discounts are organised in three ways

  • Mode
    You can choose if the discount percent is applied to each individual fee or the lowest fee amount.
  • Number or Fees
    A percentage discounted according to the number of different fees being applied in a single registration.
  • Prompt Payment Discount
    A prompt payment discount will be applied if the fee is paid in full before its due date.


Adding a 'Registration Discount' according to the number of fees

  1. Hover on 'Fees' and click 'Discounts' on the control bar at the top.
  2. Using the 'Mode' dropdown choose how the discount will be applied.
  3. Insert a minimum 'Number of Fees' a registrar needs in order to get the discount.
  4. Insert a number for the 'Discount' percentage.
  5. Check or uncheck the box to disable or enable the discount if a members fees are payed after the due date set on the term fee.
  6. Click 'Save'.

Adding a 'Prompt Payment Discount'

  1. Hover on 'Fees' and click 'Discounts' on the control bar at the top.
  2. Using the 'Mode' dropdown choose how the discount will be applied.
  3. Insert a number for the 'Discount' percentage.
  4. Check or uncheck the box to disable or enable the discount if a members fees are payed after the due date set on the term fee.
  5. Click 'Save'.

Add a New Member Fee

  1. Hover on 'Fees'. 
  2. Click 'Term Fees'. 
  3. The top panel is where you create new member fee.
  4. Click the first drop box and at the bottom click 'New Member Fee'.
  5. Select the code you are applying the fee to. If the same fee applies to multiple codes you can select as many as you like.
  6. If you have Xero enabled you may be asked to choose your fee category.
  7. Set the due date or days. 
  8. Enter the amount.  
  9. Click 'Add'.

    If the the button is not clickable this means the data has not been filled in correctly.
